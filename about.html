Our Mission

At PennyJuice, we believe in making nutrition fun, vibrant, and delicious. Our juice concentrates are specially formulated for kids, with no added sugar or artificial ingredients—just pure, fruity goodness!

We proudly serve childcare centers, preschools, and families nationwide. Our mission is to provide a cost-effective and healthy beverage solution that children love and parents trust.

PennyJuice is committed to quality, convenience, and care. We're more than just a beverage company—we're your partner in children's nutrition.

Our Story

PennyJuice was founded in 2001 with a mission to support children's health in childcare environments. What began as a small family business in Las Vegas quickly grew into a trusted supplier of nutritious juice concentrates.

Today, PennyJuice proudly serves thousands of childcare centers and families across the country, offering healthy, delicious, and affordable juice solutions that children love and parents trust.

From our humble beginnings, we’ve remained dedicated to our vision: providing high-quality, convenient, and nutritious beverages for children everywhere.
